# GDPR Compliance Statement

**Last updated:** June 2026  
**Project:** Whisper of the Veil  
**Studio:** ThetaB Studios  

---

## 1. Overview

ThetaB Studios is committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applicable within the European Union.

This document explains how user data is handled in relation to *Whisper of the Veil* and associated services.

---

## 2. Data Collection

ThetaB Studios does **not collect, store, or process personal data** from users in the context of the game.

No user accounts, profiles, or personal identification systems are required.

---

## 3. Legal Basis

Since no personal data is collected, there is no processing of personal data under GDPR definitions.

Therefore, GDPR rights are inherently respected by design.

---

## 4. User Rights Under GDPR

Under GDPR, users typically have the right to:

- Access their personal data  
- Request correction of data  
- Request deletion of data  
- Restrict or object to processing  
- Data portability  

As ThetaB Studios does not store personal data, these rights are not actively processed but remain fully respected.

---

## 5. Data Security

Any technical or operational data used (if applicable) is:

- Non-personal  
- Temporary  
- Not stored long-term  
- Not used for profiling or tracking  

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

Third-party services, if used, are limited strictly to essential infrastructure (e.g., hosting platforms) and are not used for behavioral tracking or advertising purposes.

---

## 7. Children’s Data

No personal data is knowingly collected from children or any users.

---

## 8. Changes to This Statement

This GDPR statement may be updated in the future to reflect legal or technical changes.

---

## 9. Contact

For GDPR-related inquiries, please contact ThetaB Studios via official communication channels.
