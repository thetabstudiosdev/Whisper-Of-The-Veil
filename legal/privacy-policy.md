# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026  
**Project:** Whisper of the Veil  
**Studio:** ThetaB Studios  

---

## 1. Overview

ThetaB Studios is committed to protecting user privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled in relation to the game *Whisper of the Veil* and any associated services.

---

## 2. Data Collection

ThetaB Studios does **not collect, store, sell, or share personal user data**.

We do not require user accounts, registrations, or personal profiles to access or play the game.

---

## 3. Data Usage

In cases where technical data may be processed, it is strictly limited to:

- Game functionality  
- Performance optimization  
- Error detection and debugging  

This data is non-personal and cannot be used to identify individual users.

---

## 4. Data Storage

- No personal data is stored on external servers  
- No tracking of user behavior is implemented  
- No user profiling occurs  

All data processing is temporary and used only for operational purposes.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

ThetaB Studios does not use advertising networks or data-selling analytics systems.

Third-party tools may only be used for essential infrastructure purposes (e.g., hosting platforms).

---

## 6. GDPR Compliance (European Union)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users have rights including:

- Right to access personal data  
- Right to correct data  
- Right to delete data  
- Right to restrict processing  
- Right to data portability  
- Right to object to processing  

Since ThetaB Studios does not collect personal data, these rights are respected by design.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The game is not intended for children under the minimum legal age in their jurisdiction.

No personal data from minors is knowingly collected.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected in this document.

---

## 9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact via the official ThetaB Studios communication channels.
